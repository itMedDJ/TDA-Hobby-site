What is final fantasy 14 about?

That is a good question. to sum it up becuase im new and havent done much of the story. you are the warrior of light and becuase of that you are the realms only hope in stopping the bad dudes from being bad dudes. as you play the game you unlock new jobs (this games version of classes) and they vary wildly in what they can do.

Final Fantasy 14

The info i have on the gameplay is going to be super limited as i havent really played enough to have experienced it. that being said what i have played is awesome. the gameplay is very reminiscent of games like wow but with a JRPG twist that im sure fans of either franchise will love. the battles range from this is okay to, why does the floor hate me, which is rather engaging because the game tells you where the bad part to stand is and then you will have reposition or your healer calls you names. the one thing that stands out in final fantasy 14 however is the comunity of players, i have never been apart of a gaming comunity this friendly ever, as a new player other players where so kind and under standing that you were new that they just helped and quested with you not because they got anything from it but because they wanted to my free company are also some of the nicest people ever and in the first 10 mins of being in it they queued raids and dugeons with me just to helo the queue timer which would never happen in other titles.

The classes

This game has 19 different classes for the player to choose from. these classes fit into a sublass of either the tank, healer, melee dps, ranged dps or caster dps. because of the amount of classes in the game im just going to talk about the ones i have actually played.

The red mage

The red mage was the second class i picked up and let me tell you, it is so much fun. the red mages gimick is something called dual cast. what this does is when you cast a spell you get to cast another one with zero cool down so the gameplay loop is cast a short cool down and then a long one with no charge. on top of this you have two mana bars to track. the black and white mana bars fill when you cast certain spells and once you have a certain ammount you can use melee combos.

The gunbreaker

The gunbreaker is a tank that moonlights as a dps. they excel at good damage and good self healing. there main mechanic is there weapon charges that you obtain through certain combos. once obtained you can use them to pull off high damage combos that are really quite flashy for a tank. the gunbreaker is my favourite tank in the game to play and may be my favourite class in general.
