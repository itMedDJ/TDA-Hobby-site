So you want to hear a story eh?

The borderlands franchise started off as a "what if diablo had a metric ass ton of guns?" and has turned into a well known franchsie in the gaming space. The premise is that there are things called vaults on planets that house both loot and vault monsters. you play as one of many vault hunters and your task is simple, find the vault, stop the bad guy and get some sweet loot in the process.

Borderlands

The gameplay is very similar to destiny in that you kill a bad guy to get a weapon to then kill another bad guy to get a bigger gun and so on. the differences come from the charecters you play. in the borderlands franchise you pick from one of many different achatypes of charecter ranging from sneaky assassin with either a melee or a sniper rifle or a short guy who can use 2 guns at once (would you be shocked if the latter was the most broken?). in the universe there are also sirens. these sirens are the closest thing to a mage you get in this franchise, a few key notes here are that only 7 can exist and that all sirens are AFAB.

The biggest draw to borderlands

without question the biggest draw to the borderlands franchise is the sheer amount of guns you can obtain, ranging from your standard pistol to a laucher that shoots burgers (yes really burgers).

My favourite weapons

Some of my favourite weapons in the franchise include the light show from borderlands 3, the hellfire from borderlands 1 and the unkempt harold from borderlnads 2
