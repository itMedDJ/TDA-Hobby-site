What is destiny about?

Destiny 2 at its core is a game about loot and killing bigger and bigger bad guys. The story of destiny is a rather complcated space opera about a world where the traveler has come to the solar system that then lead to humanity experiencing a goldend age. life spans increased exponentially and tehcnology get better and better. What the solar system didnt know is that a calamity was on the horizon. as for the current story we are at the end of the light and darkness saga where the very fate of humanity and the universe is at stake, friends and enemys have come together to fight our biggest enemy ever. The witness.

In the next expansion to the game and the last one in the light and dark saga. we will finally face off agaisnt the witness and see if we have what it takes to save the universe or will the witness achieve the final shape and mold the universe into a dead and bleak world.

Destiny 2

The gameplay of destiny 2, to this day, this day has some of the best feeling gameplay in the FPS space. But coming from the original creators of halo, who is suprised? the things the player has to do is rather alot. between strikes, seasonal activities, PVP, gambit and raids the player will have plenty of things to get upto in the very varied atmospheres the game offers. speaking of atmospheres, destinys level design is some of the best in the gaming space. between the EDZ and the dreaming city, you will be taken aback at just how beautiful the game is.

As for the reason why the world looks as good as it does. the traveler terrorformed many planets in our system so that they can sustauin life.

The classes

The classes of destiny 2 are the Warlock, titan and hunter. each class has its own design philosophy.

The warlock

Warlocks are your space wizards, comanding the very elements to either destroy the very attoms your enemys are made from or supply the team wtih support like healing or increased damage output. you will often find them reading about best to harness the very fundamentals of the universe to destroy their foes.

The titan

The titans are the most straight forward class in the game, both with there kit and thinking. titans love to punch things and ask the questions late such as "whyd that guy explode?". this class is one of the best classes to play if you like dealing the highest DPS and living to tell the tale, all of there subclasses have ways to either sustain themselves or to take less damage. the standout class here is void, it offers the titan the ability to support their team with the ward of dawn.

The hunter

Hunter are known for two things in the destiny comunity, jumping and being flimsy. Dont let that fool you though no fireteam is complete without one. the have access to some of the best weapon dps in the game thanks to there dodge ability and exotic armors that add some cool utility to the class. much like titan, hunter is a very solo focussed class with the option to use a tether that does two things, increses the damage enemys take and share damage between them. Very handy in raids.
